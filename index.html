About Sheefah Zarma

Shareefah Ibrahim Zarma, known professionally as Sheefah Zarma, is an acclaimed Nigerian poet, author, and powerful spoken word artist. A dedicated advocate for social change, she leverages her artistic platforms to address critical issues such as religious bigotry and tribalism, championing a vision for a more unified, inclusive, and compassionate society. Through her two published works, 'The Silent Noise' and 'Black Rose & Other Poems', and her dynamic stage performances, Sheefah gives voice to the pressing need for dialogue and understanding, transforming creative expression into a catalyst for positive impact.

Career Highlights & Engagements

Sheefah Zarma is a sought-after voice who has been invited to perform and speak at numerous high-profile conferences and events, where her work resonates with diverse audiences. Her notable engagements include:
