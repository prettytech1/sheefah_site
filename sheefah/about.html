Leadership & Advocacy

Beyond her artistic endeavors, Sheefah has demonstrated a profound commitment to activism through strategic leadership. She formerly served as the Chairperson of National Unity Advocates, a non-governmental organization dedicated to fostering peace and solidarity across Nigeria. In this role, she spearheaded initiatives and dialogues aimed at bridging cultural and religious divides.

Sheefah Zarma is also trailblazing CEO in the health and fitness industry with a deep-rooted passion for healthy living. Hailing from Maiduguri, Nigeria, she brings a global perspective shaped by her upbringing and cultural values, she advocates for holistic wellness on a global scale. Her journey towards optimal health extends far beyond the physical realm, recognizing the pivotal role of mental well-being and believing that a healthy mind is essential for a healthy body. As the CEO of SnapBack, Sheefah Zarma leads by example, embodying the values of holistic wellness and inspiring others to prioritize their health. Driven by her unwavering dedication to holistic wellness, Zarma continues to push boundaries and redefine the standards of health and fitness and has invested not only financially but also mentally, intellectually, and physically. Her journey serves as a testament to the transformative power of prioritizing both physical and mental health in achieving overall well-being. Zarma also advocates for the integration of yoga and meditation into daily routines, promoting inner peace and resilience alongside physical fitness
